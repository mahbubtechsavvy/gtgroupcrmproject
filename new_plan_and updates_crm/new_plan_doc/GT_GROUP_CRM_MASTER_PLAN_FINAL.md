# 🏆 FINAL UNIFIED MASTER PLAN — GT GROUP CRM
**Version:** 5.0 (Final Production Plan)
**Date:** April 16, 2026

This is the definitive, single source of truth for the GT Group CRM project. It consolidates all past development phases, current codebase realities, and the exact requirements specified from the notebook and user feedback.

---

## 🏗️ 1. CORE ARCHITECTURE & INFRASTRUCTURE
*   **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
*   **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
*   **Styling:** CSS Modules with GT Group Premium "Dark Navy & Gold" Aesthetic.
*   **Security:** Deep Row-Level Security (RLS). Super Admins have full CRUD bypass and global control over all offices.
*   **Data Entry UX:** All forms feature dynamic "Example Answer" placeholders (e.g., *e.g., Apple MacBook Air M2*) before typing to guide staff perfectly.

---

## 🆔 2. IDENTITY, ROLES & PERMISSIONS
*   **Automated Employee ID:** Every time a Super Admin creates a new user, a unique 8-digit number (e.g., `82710394`) is auto-generated by the CRM database.
    *   This ID is visible in the Settings > Account section.
    *   It is used in APIs to connect and verify office members.
    *   It operates dynamically in the Super Admin Dashboard search engine.
*   **Permissions System:** Controlled globally from the `/settings/permissions` page. Super Admins wield ultimate control, ensuring permission structures work flawlessly across the multi-office structure.
*   **Global Off-boarding:** Staff can be deactivated instantly by Super Admins.

---

## 📧 3. COMMUNICATION & CALENDAR CORE
*   **Google Meet Integration:** Online events auto-generate a unique 42-character Meet link. Events are pushed to both CRM local calendars and synced to Student/Counselor Google Calendars via API (`/api/google-calendar-sync`).
*   **Super Admin Email Template Editor:** Modeled after Supabase's template editor. Super Admins can edit HTML source code, define subjects, use dynamic placeholders (e.g., `{{student_name}}`), and view live previews of system emails (like Invoices or Event Invitations).
*   **Smart Routing:** Incoming emails are routed directly to the specific student's profile via `emailRouter.js`.

---

## 🏢 4. THE SIX NEW PRODUCTION MODULES

### A. GT Group All Office Work Schedule Management
*   **Scope:** Not just HR, but an all-office overarching schedule management system.
*   **Features:**
    *   Real-time Clock-in / Clock-out.
    *   Leave requests, holiday tracking, and overtime calculation.
    *   Manager verification workflows.
    *   Dashboard widget: **Smart Daily Schedule** splitting work into *Important Work*, *Today*, and *Tomorrow*.

### B. GT Group Contact Network
*   **Scope:** Global corporate directory for staff and partners.
*   **Features:**
    *   Contact Cards showing Name, auto-generated Employee ID, Phone, and DB Photo.
    *   Expanded Social Media options: LinkedIn, Facebook, WhatsApp, X (Twitter), Instagram.
    *   Export capabilities to PDF or Excel for single contacts, office groups, or the entire network.

### C. Office Inventory Management
*   **Features:**
    *   Asset tracking (Item Name, Specs, Quantity, Purchase Date).
    *   Assignment tracking (Location, Person in Charge).
    *   Condition tracking (Active / In Repair / Disposed).
    *   Export reports to PDF/Excel.
    *   Strict placeholder guidance to ensure staff enter specifications correctly.

### D. Office Visitor Log
*   **Scope:** Comprehensive security log for all incoming office guests.
*   **Features:**
    *   Tracks Visitor Name, Purpose, and assigns a Host Staff member.
    *   Real-time "In Office" / "Checked Out" status.
    *   *Super Admin Access:* Super Admins can view, edit, and add visitors across all office logs globally.

### E. Financial Track 1: Monthly Expense Reports
*   **Scope:** Strict auditing of past expenditures.
*   **Features:**
    *   Submission locked to the 30th / End of Month.
    *   Tracks Income vs. Expense by line item.
    *   Audit logged to the specific "Fund User" (the person who handled the money).
    *   Must be Approved/Rejected by the Super Admin.

### F. Financial Track 2: Expenditure Planning
*   **Scope:** Future budget forecasting.
*   **Features:**
    *   Requests grouped by Position/Department and Priority.
    *   Estimated Cost aggregation for the upcoming month.
    *   Sent to Super Admin for budget approval decisions.
