## 🚀 Final Phase Accomplishments

### 📅 Smart Google Calendar Sync
The CRM is now officially "Smart." When a counselor creates an "Online" meeting:
- **Auto-Meet:** A unique Google Meet link is generated.
- **Sync:** The event is pushed to the Counselor's Google Calendar.
- **Invite:** The Student receives an automated email and a calendar invite.
- **Dashboard Widget:** All meetings are visible in the new **Daily Schedule** priority tabs (*Important / Today / Tomorrow*).
